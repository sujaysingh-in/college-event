ABOUT COLLEGE EVENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Event management is the application of project management to the creation and development of large-scale events such as festivals, conferences, and ceremonies.

The process includes budgeting, scheduling, site selection, and acquiring necessary permits. Sustainable event management is now a global standard, ensuring professional coordination and smooth execution.
